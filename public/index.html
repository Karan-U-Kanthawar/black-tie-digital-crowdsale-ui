INTRODUCING THE B4REAL STAKING SYSTEM

Our proprietary staking system uses blockchain technology to allow buyers interested in purchasing physical real estate to send our B4RE tokens to a staking contract which then produces a 20% yield in the form of B4RC tokens.

 

The B4RC tokens earned from staking can be used directly with our leading real estate developers.

  • Earned B4RC tokens are always worth $1 AUD with our developers;

  • Yields generated far exceed any high-interest savings account with fiat;

  • This reduces the amount of time it takes to enter the real estate market by getting you deposit ready faster;

  • Outside of using your yields on real estate transactions, you can also utilise our secondary market to sell discounted B4RC tokens for AUD or other cryptocurrencies to people ready to become property owners now.

Who is the B4REAL Staking System for?

  • For Australians wanting to buy real estate in Australia who would like to be purchase-ready faster from staking;
  • For Australian crypto-enthusiasts who'd like to profit from passively earning B4RC tokens to sell on the secondary market to people wanting to buy real estate now but not participate in staking;
  • For international crypto-enthusiasts who would like to purchase real estate in Australia; and
  • For international crypto-enthusiasts who would like to profit from passively earning B4RC tokens to sell on the secondary market to people wanting to buy real estate now but not participate in staking.
